# Module 10: Groups - The Legal Moves

## Core Question

What transformations are allowed?

## Limitation

Many objects matter less as static things and more through their symmetries. We need a language for legal moves that can be composed and undone.

## New Object

A group: a set with a composition operation satisfying closure, associativity, identity, and inverses.

## Legal Operations

- Compose moves.
- Undo moves.
- Study subgroups.
- Let a group act on a set or space.
- Classify objects up to symmetry.

## Invariants

- Order of a group
- Order of an element
- Orbits
- Stabilizers
- Conjugacy classes
- Normal subgroups

## Concrete Example

The rotations of a square form a group. Rotating by 90 degrees four times returns to the identity.

## Hand Exercises

1. List the four rotational symmetries of a square.
2. Find the order of a 90-degree rotation.
3. Explain why inverses are essential for symmetry.

## Depends On

Functions, composition, and examples of transformations.

## Supports Later

Representation theory, geometry, topology, Galois theory, and category theory.
